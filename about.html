ABOUT YARN DREAMS


Yarn Dreams is a humble online store established in 2022 and based in Caloocan, dedicated to crafting you the most elegant, high-quality crocheted accessories, crop tops, stuffed toys and more.

Our store is consists of items that are unique, durable, and aesthetically satisfying. Perfect for spicing up an OOTD, adding a pop of color to any given room, or gifting to the special people in your life.

With our 100% hand-stitched, made-to-order system, we create our products with only you and your vision in mind, ensuring that every piece is a perfect balance between what you want and what we can provide.
